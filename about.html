About Care Weave

Founded in 2005, Care Weave A Context-Aware Healthcare Platform has grown into one of the region’s most trusted healthcare systems. The platform is designed to combine technology, intelligence, and compassion to deliver high-quality medical services.

Care Weave focuses on improving patient experience, reducing manual workload, and enabling informed decision making through digital healthcare services.

Our Vision

To create a smart and connected healthcare ecosystem that ensures quality care and accessibility for everyone.

Our Mission

To deliver efficient, secure, and technology-driven healthcare services with patient safety as the highest priority.

Core Values

Compassion, integrity, innovation, teamwork, and continuous improvement form the foundation of Care Weave.
